Area Highlights

What's in each direction

🏄 Pleasure Point Corridor (walking distance)

World-class surf break steps from the house. The 41st Ave hub has coffee, pizza, surf shops, craft beer, and a donut shop — all under 0.7 miles. The Hook County Park is 0.24 miles.

🌺 Capitola Village (~1 mile)

Colorful beachfront village with Zelda's on the Beach, Pizza My Heart, boutique shops, and the iconic Capitola Beach. Best visited early before parking gets brutal on holiday weekends.

🎢 Santa Cruz Boardwalk (3.5 miles)

Classic amusement park with the historic Looff Carousel (top toddler pick), Sky Glider, and Main Beach. Free to walk — rides are ticketed. Go before 11am to beat the holiday crowds.

🌲 Redwood Day Trips (8+ miles)

Roaring Camp Railroad and Henry Cowell Redwoods are both standout toddler experiences — steam train through old-growth redwoods. Book Roaring Camp in advance.

Trip Logistics

Practical notes

🛒 Groceries & Supplies

  • 🥬 New Leaf Community Market (1.1 mi) — Best local option. Prepared foods, deli, wine and beer.
  • 🛍️ Whole Foods + Trader Joe's + Target all within 1.3–1.4 mi on the 41st Ave corridor.
  • ⚡ El Rancho Shopping Center (0.5 mi) is the fastest run for coffee, donuts, pizza, or last-minute items.
  • 🍖 Pointside Meat Shop (0.68 mi) for craft beer, meat, and snacks — walkable.

🚗 Parking Tips

  • ⏰ Capitola Beach and the Boardwalk fill fast on holiday weekends — arrive by 9–10am or walk in from a paid lot.
  • 🚶 The Pleasure Point / 41st Ave strip is walkable from home, saving the car for longer drives.
  • 🌅 Rio Del Mar (3.1 mi east) has easier parking than Santa Cruz beaches on busy weekends.

👶 Toddler Tips

  • 🏞️ Moran Lake County Park is the standout — lagoon, beach access, restrooms, mellow energy.
  • 🎠 Boardwalk Carousel (Looff Carousel) is the best toddler ride — go early before it gets crowded.
  • 🚂 Roaring Camp Railroad is a must if doing a day trip — train through redwoods is a genuine experience.
  • 🚶 East Cliff Drive Pathway is fully stroller-friendly with ocean views the whole way.
